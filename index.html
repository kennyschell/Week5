Momo

This is my cat Mo, she is my favourite pet.
Shh, don't tell the others.
We believe Mo was a wizard in a past life. She's very intuitive and loves witchcraft.
Mo was abandoned and left for dead as a young kitten. The rescue group found her in near death condition.
I'll leave out the hideous details, but our sweet girl had to have both of her eyes removed.
Being blind doesn't stop Momo, though! She's an absolute angel, and an important part of our family!
